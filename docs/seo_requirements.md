# SEO Dominance Requirements

## Goal
Dominate SEO for floor preparation services in South East Queensland.
Tone: Expert but friendly, focusing on high-end work and legacy craftsmanship.

## Target Pages

### Services
- Floor Preparation
- Concrete Grinding
- Floor Levelling / Self-Levelling
- Moisture Barriers
- Subfloor Repairs
- Adhesive & Glue Removal (tile, carpet, timber, vinyl)
- Epoxy Removal
- Industrial Surface Preparation

### Locations
- Brisbane
- Gold Coast
- Sunshine Coast
- Toowoomba
- Gympie
*Format: "Floor Preparation [Location]" style.*

## Page Structure
Each page must include:
1.  **Hero:** Outcome framing (mirror-flat floors, perfect substrates).
2.  **Why Proper Floor Prep Matters:** Educational value.
3.  **Our Process:** Step-by-step with technical terminology.
4.  **Suitable For:** Residential, commercial, industrial.
5.  **Common Substrates We Fix:** Specific list.
6.  **Tools, Machines & Materials:** Showcase equipment.
7.  **Before & After Results:** Visual proof.
8.  **CTAs:** Phone, Quote Form, Email.
9.  **SEO & FAQ:** 5–8 specific FAQs.
10. **Local Intent:** Radius wording.
11. **Structured Data:** LocalBusiness + Service schema.

## Technical SEO
-   H1/H2/H3 hierarchy.
-   **Internal Linking:** Between service and location pages.
-   **Keywords:** “floor preparation”, “floor levelling”, “concrete grinding”, “substrate correction”, “self levelling compound”, “moisture mitigation”.
-   **No keyword stuffing:** Natural writing.
